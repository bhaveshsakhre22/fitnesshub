What is Yoga?

Yoga is an ancient practice that connects the mind, body, and breath through physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation. It's ideal for reducing stress and improving overall well-being.

Benefits of Yoga

  • Increases Flexibility: Lengthens muscles and joints
  • Improves Posture: Strengthens the spine and core alignment
  • Boosts Mental Clarity: Calms the mind and improves focus
