Top 5 Protein Sources for Muscle Growth üçóüí™

1. Chicken Breast

Protein: ~31g per 100g

Why: Lean, low-fat, and one of the most complete animal proteins. A go-to for muscle builders.

How to Use: Grilled, baked, or shredded in salads and wraps.

2. Eggs

Protein: ~6g per large egg

Why: Contains all 9 essential amino acids and healthy fats in the yolk. Great for muscle recovery.

How to Use: Boiled, scrambled, or in omelets with veggies.

3. Greek Yogurt

Protein: ~10g per 100g

Why: Offers both whey (fast-acting) and casein (slow-absorbing) protein for sustained recovery.

How to Use: Enjoy plain, in smoothies, or with berries and honey.

4. Whey Protein Powder

Protein: ~24g per 30g scoop

Why: Quickly absorbed and perfect post-workout. Packed with BCAAs.

How to Use: Mix with water or milk within 30 minutes of training.

5. Salmon

Protein: ~25g per 100g

Why: High in quality protein and omega-3s, helping muscle recovery and inflammation reduction.

How to Use: Bake or grill and serve with rice and greens.
