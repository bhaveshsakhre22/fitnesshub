What is Pilates?

Pilates is a low-impact workout focused on strengthening core muscles, improving flexibility, posture, and overall body awareness through controlled movements.

Benefits of Pilates

  • Improves Core Strength: Builds a strong foundation for better movement
  • Enhances Flexibility: Lengthens muscles and increases joint mobility
  • Reduces Injury Risk: Focus on control and alignment protects the body

Sample Pilates Routine

  • The Hundred
  • Roll-Up
  • Leg Circles
  • Single-Leg Stretch
  • Plank to Push-Up
